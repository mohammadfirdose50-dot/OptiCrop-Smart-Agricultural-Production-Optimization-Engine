📖 About OptiCrop

Learn more about our mission to revolutionize agriculture with AI.

🌾 Our Mission

OptiCrop is a Smart Agricultural Production Optimization Engine that uses Machine Learning to recommend the best crops based on soil nutrients and environmental conditions. We help farmers maximize yields, optimize resources, and make data-driven decisions.

🧠 How It Works

Our AI model analyzes 7 key parameters:

  • Nitrogen (N) - Essential for leaf growth
  • Phosphorous (P) - Important for root development
  • Potassium (K) - Crucial for overall plant health
  • Temperature - Affects crop growth rate
  • Humidity - Influences water requirements
  • pH Level - Determines nutrient availability
  • Rainfall - Affects water supply

🎯 Our Impact

By providing accurate crop recommendations, OptiCrop helps:

  • 🌱 Increase crop yields by 20-30%
  • 💧 Optimize water and fertilizer usage
  • 🌍 Promote sustainable farming practices
  • 📊 Enable data-driven agricultural decisions
  • 💰 Maximize farmer profitability
